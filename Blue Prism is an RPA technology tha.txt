Blue Prism is an RPA technology that uses software robots to implement the logic of a business process1. Each robot emulates a single human user, accessing applications just as a person would, and each one runs in its own Windows virtual machine1. Blue Prism automates repetitive, rule-based, manual back-office tasks, making use of a 'Digital Workforce'2. The tool offers a flow chart like a designer with drag and drops features for automating each step of the business process3. Blue Prism processes are flow diagrams that are graphical representations of the process the computer follows
